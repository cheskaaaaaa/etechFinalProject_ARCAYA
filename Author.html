About the author

Mò Xiāng Tóng Xiù, is a highly influencial

writer in the danmei genre. She wrote and

published "The Scum Villian's Self-saving

System", "Grandmaster of Demonic

Cultivation", and "Heaven Official's Blessing"

originally in a Chinese web novel site called

JJWXC.

As all three webnovels boomed in popularity,

all were adapted to manhua, with an

addition of "Heaven Official's Blessing"

donghua adaptation.
