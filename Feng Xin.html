Feng Xin, titled as General Nan Yang, is the Martial God who oversees the Southeast and has nearly eight thousand temples dedicated to his palace. Feng Xin is known to be polite, courteous and honest with a strong sense of responsibility, not being the type to gossip or talk bad behind people's backs. He's straightforward and doesn't have much patience, being a man of quick temper with a tendency to curse loudly and excessively even when around fellow Heavenly Officials.
