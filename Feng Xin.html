Feng Xin

风信

Title: General Nan Yang

Species: God (former Human)

Sex: Male

Age: 800+ years

Birthday: Unknown

Height: 188cm

Affiliation: Heavenly Court

Feng Xin is the Martial God who oversees the Southeast and has nearly eight thousand temples dedicated to his palace. He is known to be polite, courteous and honest with a strong sense of responsibility, not being the type to gossip or talk bad behind people's backs. He's straightforward and doesn't have much patience, being a man of quick temper with a tendency to curse loudly and excessively even when around fellow Heavenly Officials.

"MU QING! YOU BASTARD, STOP RIGHT THERE!"

-Feng Xin to Mu Qing
