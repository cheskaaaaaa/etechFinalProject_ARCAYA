Xie Lian and Hua Cheng

They are the lead pair followed by the story. They first met when Hua Cheng was a child and Xie Lian played the God-Pleasing Warrior during the Heavenly Ceremonial Procession. Hua Cheng wanted to take his life that day by jumping down the city walls during the parade, but he became enchanted when he was saved by Xie Lian.
