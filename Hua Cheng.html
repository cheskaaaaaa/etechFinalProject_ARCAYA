Hua Cheng

花城

Title: Crimson Rain Sought Flower; City Master

Species: Ghost (former Human)

Sex: Male

Age: 800+ years

Birthday: June 10

Height: 190cm

Affiliation: Ghost City

He's a ghost of Supreme rank and the most notorious of the Four Great Calamities, widely known as Crimson Rain Sought Flower (血雨探花, Xuè Yǔ Tàn Huā). As a Supreme, Hua Cheng possesses an extremely powerful shape-shifting ability not even gods can see through. It's said that no one has ever seen his true form because he transformed so often.
